- Trained EM Ed how to use [Descript](https://descript.com) and hacked together an Outlook calendar-to-Trello automation on Tech Develops after watching Nat and Tzveta's amazing dyslexia talk
- Two office visits along with the first (remote) engineer interview from a meeting room since the pandemic
