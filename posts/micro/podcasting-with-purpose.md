We had a really thought provoking conversation about podcast content earlier, balancing what an audience actually wants to hear against what you want to say.  Whilst it’s tempting to lean into the former, be careful not to compromise on your values, unique personality and why you started the podcast.  People tend to follow people, personality and passion, not necessarily topics.
