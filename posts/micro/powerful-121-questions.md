<p>These questions are very powerful for scheduled 121’s with direct reports to encourage healthy relationships and conversations. </p>
<ul><li>What’s on your mind this week?</li><li>Last time we spoke, you said X was a challenge for you. How is that going?</li><li>What are your plans and priorities for this week?</li><li>How aligned do you feel with where the company is going?</li><li>What feedback do you have for me?</li></ul>
