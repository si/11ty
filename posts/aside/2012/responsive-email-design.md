Campaign Monitor have compiled a fantastic guide to [responsive email design](http://www.campaignmonitor.com/guides/mobile/).

It's broken down to fundamental techniques, recommended coding conventions with special areas on layouts and images.

The [case study](http://www.campaignmonitor.com/guides/mobile/casestudy/) they applied to Twitter's email notifications is a shining example of how easy it can be to apply quick fixes to completely revolutionise your mobile email campaign.

Don't undervalue your email campaigns - give them some responsive love.
