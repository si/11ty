[iconmonstr](http://iconmonstr.com/) is a lovely site by Alexander Kahlkopf offering free regular monochrome icons.

Icons are posted on a regular basis as PNG raster and SVG vector files and free to use on personal or commercial projects (although attribution is welcomed where possible).

New icons are simultaneously posted on the [@iconmonstr](http://twitter.com/iconmonstr) Twitter account so it's a good idea to follow that account.
