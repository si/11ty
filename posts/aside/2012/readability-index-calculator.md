How readable is your copy? Apply the Flesch-Kincaid formula to your text with the [Readability Index Calculator](http://www.standards-schmandards.com/exhibits/rix/index.php) to find out.

This tool is really useful for testing your marketing content but remember it's only a formula "“ always ask a real person if your content makes sense too. User testing and feedback is invaluable for this process.
