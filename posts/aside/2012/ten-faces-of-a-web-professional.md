Before Christmas, I was honoured to be asked to write an article for a new website called [12 Devs of Xmas](http://www.12devsofxmas.co.uk/) - a festive magazine for motivating the Web community to learn something new every day.

I decided to take a different angle and focus on [the many faces of a typical Web professional](http://www.12devsofxmas.co.uk/2012/01/the-many-faces-of-freelancing/), highlighting the broad range of skills required when working as a freelancer.
