International finance powerhouse Mastercard have recently commissioned design agency Pentagram to freshen up their identity and brand system.

It's certainly refreshing and modern whilst maintaining the iconic orange and red circles, albeit simplified - but can you imagine the effort to roll out this new identity across all their partners?
