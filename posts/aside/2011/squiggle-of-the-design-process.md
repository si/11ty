The Squiggle of the Design Process by Damien Newman at the Central Office of Design is a great representation of how designers approach new tasks, embodying the natural process we go through.

It has become so popular, they are now printing t-shirts and also have [released the illustration](http://v2.centralstory.com/about/squiggle/) for use under [Creative Commons Attribution-No Derivative Works 3.0 United States License](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nd/3.0/us/).
